Tina Nails & Spa is a private, appointment-based nail salon tucked inside a clean, peaceful suite in Glendale, CA. There are no crowds, no noise, and no waiting — only Tina, her craft, and you.

With over a decade of devoted clientele, Tina has built a reputation for precision nail art, detailed care, and the kind of unhurried attention that keeps clients coming back for years — some for more than twelve.

Whether you're stepping in for a classic manicure or a full gel set with custom designs, every appointment is quiet, personal, and worth every minute.
